Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

PGMN ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PGMN Ads platform, which helps manage and launch advertising campaigns on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may access the following information through the Meta Marketing API:

  • Facebook Page and Instagram account information associated with your ad account
  • Ad campaign data including performance metrics (impressions, reach, spend, engagements)
  • Post content (captions, thumbnails) for posts selected for promotion

We do not collect personal information from end users who view or interact with ads created through our platform.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information accessed through the Meta API solely to:

  • Create, manage, and monitor advertising campaigns on your behalf
  • Display campaign performance metrics and analytics
  • Resolve post URLs to their corresponding Meta API identifiers for ad creation

4. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your Meta account data. Campaign data is cached temporarily (up to 2 minutes) to improve performance and reduce API rate limiting. Access tokens are stored as environment variables on our secure hosting platform and are never exposed to end users.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is transmitted only between our platform and the Meta Marketing API to fulfill campaign management functions.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure environment variable storage for API credentials
  • No client-side exposure of access tokens or sensitive credentials

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke access to your Meta account at any time through your Facebook or Instagram settings
  • Request deletion of any cached data by contacting us
  • Disconnect your accounts from our platform at any time

8. Meta Platform Terms

Our use of the Meta Marketing API is governed by the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We comply with all applicable Meta data use requirements.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Facebook Page: PGMN Official.
